--- subject: Cheapass will shut down on Sunday, 1 Oct 2017 ---

Cheapass is shutting down.

It brings me a mixture of sadness and relief to announce that Cheapass will shutdown on 1st Oct 2017.


While this may come as a huge surprise to a lot of you given how well the service has been running for the last 40 months, the fact remains that Cheapass has been a one man operation and I've simply been way too busy with life and other work to give it the attention it deserves.


Of late I'd been working on revamping the entire product to match the technology with scale, and while I was at it, I realized that Cheapass doesn't make enough revenue to support it as a full time endeavour, nor does it make enough to justify the burden (financial, administrative or otherwise) of supporting it out of hours. That said, Cheapass will remain the jewel in the crown of my professional work that was done without any intentions of making it large, so I'm still exploring the possibility of one day either open sourcing it or finding a person or company that would be interested in keeping it running. If you happen to know such a person, please tell them about this. To avoid setting any unrealistic expectations this means that the website, the bookmarklet, the Android app, and the iOS app will no longer be active, maintained or supported beyond 1st Oct 2017. In fact the disruption in service has already started and you might not be able to set any more alerts or receive any further price drop alerts.


After Cheapass made its mark in this tech space, several such services have spun up and it should be easy migrating your existing alerts onto other such platforms.


The support, admiration, attention and love this project has received from each one of you has been overwhelming. If you have any questions, concerns, or anything to say in general, please feel free to email me directly at me@aakashgoel.com.


Again, thanks for all the support over the last 3 years. It's been an honour and fun helping each of you save money while shopping online.


Cheers,
Aakash Goel

PS. I'll continue building cool things. You can be the first one to know about them if you choose not to unsubscribe here.
